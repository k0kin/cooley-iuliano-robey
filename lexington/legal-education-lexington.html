Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any law schools in Lexington, Kentucky?

Yes, the University of Kentucky College of Law is located in Lexington, Kentucky.

What type of legal education can I receive at the University of Kentucky College of Law?

The University of Kentucky College of Law offers a Juris Doctor (JD) program as well as various specialized certificates and dual degrees.

Are there any other opportunities for legal education in Lexington, KY?

Yes, Transylvania University offers a pre-law program for students interested in pursuing a career in law. Additionally, various local law offices offer internships and externships to give students hands-on experience with practicing attorneys.

How long does it take to complete the JD program at the University of Kentucky College of Law?

The JD program takes three years to complete if attending full-time or four years if attending part-time.

Are there any bar admission requirements for lawyers who graduated from the University of Kentucky College of Law?

Yes, graduates must pass the bar exam administered by their respective state in order to be admitted as an attorney or lawyer within that states jurisdiction.
